This is my first post on the new site. I'm using this space to document my personal development projects — the things I build, the things I break, and everything I learn along the way.

## Why a blog?

I've always believed that the best way to solidify what you learn is to write it down. A blog forces you to:

1. **Understand** the topic well enough to explain it
2. **Structure** your thoughts coherently
3. **Share** what you've learned with others

Plus, it's nice to have a record of the things you've built. Future me will thank past me for taking notes.
